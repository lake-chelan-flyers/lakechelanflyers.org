Lake Chelan Flyers is a nonprofit chapter of the [US Hang Gliding and
Paragliding Association](http://ushpa.org/) (USHPA) supporting paragliding and
hang gliding on Chelan Butte. We faciliate access to Chelan Butte for
experienced paraglider and hang glider pilots. If you are interested in learning
to fly, please visit
[USHPA](https://www.ushpa.org/USHPA/Learn_to_fly/Become_a_pilot/Public/LearnToFly/become-a-pilot.aspx)
for more information.
