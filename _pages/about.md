Lake Chelan Flyers (LCF) is a nonprofit chapter club of the [United States Hang
Gliding and Paragliding Association](https://ushpa.org/) (USHPA) serving pilots,
both local and visiting, who fly Chelan Butte. LCF provides ongoing management
and maintenance of the Chelan Butte flying site, and serves as liaison between
the flying community and the multiple land owning agencies and other parties
involved.

The mission of LCF is to promote hang gliding and paragliding in the Lake Chelan
area.

Our activities include:
* Maintaining this website as a resource for pilots and community members.
* Coordinating with WA DNR and Chelan PUD for continued access to our launches
  and landings.
* Site maintenance such as mowing, trimming brush, placing wind indicators, and
  occasional road repairs.
* Acquiring and maintaining fire suppression equipment as required at launch by
  DNR for organized events.
* Renewing our insurance with [RRRG](https://www.recreationrrg.com/) on an
  annual basis.
