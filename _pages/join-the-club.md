**Annual Membership Dues: $30/year**

As a club we are responsible for the maintenance and insurance of the Chelan
Butte launches and landing zones. We work with several public landowners to
ensure continued access to these lands for freeflight. Your dues help us cover
the costs involved in keeping this incredible flying site open!

Membership is valid for one full year. Renewing early will extend your
expiration date by one year. Make sure to fill out the form every year!
