Below are several weather resources that pilots may find useful when evaluating
conditions.

## Observations

- [LCF Weather Station](https://chelancam.soaringpotato.com/) (currently offline)
- [WA DNR Panoramic Camera](https://wadnr.wildfirewatch.com/station/3290b7ab65ec4cc28b5d81308c04bc82)

## Forecasts

- [National Weather Service](https://forecast.weather.gov/MapClick.php?lon=-120.038&lat=47.803)
- [Windgrams](http://wxtofly.net/v2/windgrams.html#Chelan)
- [Windy](https://www.windy.com/?47.822,-120.007,11)
- [SkySight](https://skysight.io/) (Paid service)
- [XC Skies](https://xcskies.com/) (Paid service)
