Lake Chelan Flyers manages, maintains, and insures the historic Chelan Butte
launches and landing zones. We have hosted some of the most spectacular
freeflight in the country for over 40 years. All donations go to supporting and
preserving freeflight on Chelan Butte and the surrounding area.

Consider [joining the club](/join-the-club/) instead of or in addition to
supporting us with a donation. While club membership is intended mainly for
rated pilots who fly Chelan Butte, we welcome non-pilot members as well.

Thank you so much for supporting this very special place.
