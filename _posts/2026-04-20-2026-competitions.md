Chelan Butte is hosting two back-to-back Cat 2 FAI paragliding competitions this
June.

**[Ozone Chelan Open 2026](https://airtribune.com/ozone-chelan-open-2026/info)**
— June 13 (practice) and June 14–19 (competition). EN-C and lower, aimed at XC
pilots new to racing. Daily briefings cover task analysis and racing strategy
alongside elite pilots.

**[US Open of Paragliding
2026](https://airtribune.com/us-open-paragliding-2026/info)** — June 20
(practice) and June 21–27 (competition). Open-class Race to Goal.

For more information, including volunteer opportunities, contact [Northwest
Paragliding](https://www.nwparagliding.school/comps).
